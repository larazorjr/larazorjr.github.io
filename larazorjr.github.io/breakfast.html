    • Breakfast:Mikate

    Ingredients

    Makes Five Beignets
    • 250 g flour
    • 100 g sugar
    • 1 packet of dehydrated yeast (5g)
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 200 g lukewarm water
    • Frying oil

    Steps

    1. Mix the lukewarm water with the yeast in a small bowl. Let it sit for 5 minutes to let the yeast activate.
    2. Mix the flour, vanilla, salt and sugar in a bowl.
    3. Make a well in the middle and add the yeast/water mixture. Mix with your hands until you get a thick but smooth dough.
    4. Let it rest at least 30 minutes, preferably a couple hours.
    5. Heat the frying oil at 180°C/350°F in a fryer or a sauce pan with 1 liter of oil.
    6. drop little dollops of dough into the hot oil.
    7. Fry them for about 5 minutes, they must be golden on the outside.
    8. Get them out of the oil and let them sit on a plate with paper tower
    9. Serve warm!
