    • Congolese Cuisine

    The food history of the indigenous people is represented in the diverse cuisines of the Democratic Republic of the Congo and the Republic of the Congo. The main foods consumed with various side dishes are often cassava, fufu, rice, plantains, and potatoes. With the range of meats, vegetables, and grains that are employed in various recipes, Congolese cuisine demonstrates its adaptability.
