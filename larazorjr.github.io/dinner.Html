    • Dinner: Poisson Frit

    Ingredients

    • Whole fish
    • 1 lemon
    • Adobo
    • Bouillon cube (Maggie)
    • Oil

    Steps

    1. Wash Fish
    2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium
    3. Make slits in skin of fish then season fish
    4. Fry until cooked through and skin is deeply browned and crisp, about 4 minutes per side
